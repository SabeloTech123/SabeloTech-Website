Our Story

Who We Are

SabeloTech

SabeloTech was started with a simple observation: too many people are priced out of functional technology. A working laptop should not require months of saving. A reliable smartphone should not be a luxury.

We source second-hand phones, laptops, hard drives and RAM — inspect every single one through a 15-point diagnostic process — and sell them at prices that make sense for students, small businesses, and families.

Every device is graded honestly. Grade A means like-new. Grade B means functional with minor cosmetic wear. Grade C means it works perfectly but shows its age. No hidden surprises.

"Good tech should not be a privilege. It should be accessible to anyone willing to look a little harder."

— Sabelo, Founder
